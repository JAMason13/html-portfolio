The Best Movies and TV Shows According to Jana and Joe

Jana and Joe Review Shows and Movies!

Oppenheimer

Jana and I watched this together and really enjoyed it. We split it into two parts, 1 hour and 2 hours. The story is dark, but beautifully portrayed. We were very impressed by the use of sound throughout the movie

Barbie

We were not big fans of Barbie. The costumes and artistic vision were awesome! We were just bored for the most part, and didn't connect well with the plot

Secret Chef 'AKA Cheffy'

The game design was fun! The show was easy to watch and was about food! The chefs on the show had good personalities as well!
